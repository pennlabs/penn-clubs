Renew {{ name }} on Penn Clubs & SAC Fair Signup

Dear {{ name }} officers,

We have opened the student group registration process for the current academic year. To be considered an active student group on campus, the University requires all student organizations to be registered with the Office of Student Affairs. This year, OSA has migrated the Student Groups platform from the previous system, G.O. Penn, to Penn Labs' platform, Penn Clubs. All official registry of student groups will now be handled through Penn Clubs.

Additionally, the Student Activities Council (SAC) is partnering with Penn Clubs to host a virtual multiday Activities Fair from Sep 1 – 3. More details on the Fair will be shared at a later date by SAC. To participate in the Fair, you must be a student-run club at the University of Pennsylvania. Registration for the SAC fair is included in this years' renewal process. The deadline to sign up for the SAC Fair is August 24th.

If you have already graduated, PLEASE FORWARD THIS EMAIL to the current leadership of the group so that they may complete the registration process and sign up for the SAC Fair. If they are not currently listed as an officer for your club on Penn Clubs, they will need to email contact@pennclubs.com with their name, PennKey, and club name.

Once the request for registration is approved, the group will be listed on the front page of Penn Clubs and will be searchable for students on the site.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Since this information is being distributed through various listservs and methods, please communicate with your entire executive board before signing up for the fair. ONLY ONE officer of the club should be designated to register the group.
